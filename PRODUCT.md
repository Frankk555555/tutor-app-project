# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Thai students seeking academic tutoring, exam preparation, or skill development, along with their parents who often participate in the decision and booking process. On the other side, local educators and subject-matter experts looking to manage their schedules, profile pages, and booking requests.

## Product Purpose
The platform connects students with qualified tutors to facilitate personalized learning. It solves the pain point of finding verified, high-quality local tutors by offering robust search, rating-guided profiles, and real-time scheduling. Success is defined by seamless search-to-booking conversions and clear dashboard visibility for active lessons.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Trustworthy, Supportive. The voice is encouraging, clear, and reassuring, ensuring users feel guided and confident throughout their learning journey.

## Anti-references
- **Cluttered Directories**: Avoid cramped layouts, low-contrast text tables, generic stock imagery, and overloaded search filters that make discovery painful.
- **Dry/Clinical Enterprise SaaS**: Avoid overly cold, purely grey/blue templates that feel impersonal and lack warmth or educational encouragement.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity Over Clutter**: Minimize cognitive load by presenting tutor details and scheduler pages in clear, digestible layouts with generous white space.
- **Supportive Trust**: Elevate tutor credentials, reviews, and schedules to build instant credibility and confidence.
- **Inclusive Legibility**: Pay close attention to typography scale and spacing to ensure Thai script is perfectly readable, comfortable, and elegant.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG AA Compliance**: High-contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for standard text) for all labels, placeholders, and interactive states.
- **Thai Typography Polish**: Appropriate line-heights and font sizes specifically tuned for Thai font rendering (like Chakra Petch or prompt web fonts).
- **Reduced Motion Support**: Alternative transitions/crossfades for users with motion sensitivity.
